About Us

Established in 1995, Hi-Tech Designs Koratty has been a cornerstone of architectural innovation and engineering excellence in Kerala. Located in the Kallikadavil Complex at the NH Junction in Koratty, we have been dedicated to delivering professional and unique designs that stand out for their creativity and functionality. Under the visionary leadership of our owner, Shaju Vennookaran, our team of skilled architects collaborates to bring exceptional design concepts to life. Our commitment to quality and uniqueness ensures that our projects are distinctive and unparalleled, making us a trusted name in the industry.
